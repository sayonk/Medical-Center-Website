    About us

    The Dundurn Medical Centre Inc. operates as a family practice and a walk in clinic in Hamilton, ON

    The Dundurn Medical Centre Inc. is committed to providing high quality, medical care in a clean and friendly environment with added convenience.

    Our clinic provides both Family Practice and Walk-In style medical service where patients can be treated by a qualified health care team. We understand our patients’ needs and treat all with care and respect.
